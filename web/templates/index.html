Hirschy Factor Guide

SOS Factor

Weighs the Strength of Schedule. Higher values favor teams with battle-tested records over high win-rate teams from easier conferences.

TRB Factor

Total Rebound Percentage importance. Essential for identifying teams that dominate second-chance opportunities.

TO Factor

Turnover Percentage. Penalizes teams with poor ball security, a critical predictor in high-stakes March matchups.

Efficiency

Calculates the delta between Adjusted Offensive and Defensive efficiency. The core engine of the Hirschy model.

Momentum

Factors in late-season performance (last 10 games). Pinpoints teams that are "peaking" at the right time.
